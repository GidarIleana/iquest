The GymGenics Club has a fully equipped studio and offers a lots of classes per week. The timetable provides aerobic, muscle conditioning, sport-specific and dance-orientated class workouts as well as Pilates, Spinning, Stretch and our very own ‘Cyclebox’ classes.

The classes are designed for all levels of fitness and the coaches are highly motivated, fully trained and always aim to meet your training goals in a friendly and enjoyable atmosphere.
